# Dinner Plan at The Gannet, Glasgow

## Restaurant Information
- **Name:** The Gannet
- **Address:** 1155 Argyle Street, Finnieston, Glasgow G3 8TB
- **Phone:** 0141 204 2081

## Description
The Gannet is a modern Scottish fine dining restaurant located in Glasgow's vibrant Finnieston neighbourhood. Known for its ambitious cooking and visually impressive dishes, it offers a cozy atmosphere managed by a close-knit team of professionals. The restaurant prides itself on using the best local produce, providing an exceptional dining experience that features both a la carte and tasting menus.

## Menu Highlights
- **A La Carte Menu** 
- **6-Course Seasonal Tasting Menu:**

- **Sample Menu Items:**

## Pricing
- The average dining experience at The Gannet costs around £65 - £100 per person depending on the menu chosen. 

## Conclusion
The Gannet promises a memorable dining experience with its innovative dishes and high-quality service. Ideal for a special occasion or an elegant dining experience in Glasgow.
